Apple Japanese Cocktail

Based on Imbibe's improved Japanese cocktail. This has a bright apple flavor perfect for autumn.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. calvados
  • 1 oz. fresh lime juice
  • 1 oz. orgeat
  • 3 dashes Peychaud's bitters

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in to an ice filled shaker
  2. Shake until your hands are cold
  3. Strain in to a chilled glass
