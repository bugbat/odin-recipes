Pizza Dough Tart

Inspired by Jacques Pepin's pizza dough apple galette. Makes a great canvas for trying out different fruits and jams.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ portion of NY style pizza dough (or any pizza dough, even store bought)
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • 4tbs butter
  • Strawberries or any similar fruit
  • Any jam/jelly (I used mulberry)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450F
  2. Lightly dust pizza tray/peel with sugar
  3. Stretch prepared dough into pizza shape and place on tray/peel
  4. Slice strawberries ⅛-¼ inch thick
  5. Spread a thin layer of jam on dough
  6. Distribute strawberries evenly across dough
  7. Chop butter into small pieces and distribute evenly
  8. Top with ⅓ cup sugar
  9. Bake for ~40 minutes or until nicely browned
