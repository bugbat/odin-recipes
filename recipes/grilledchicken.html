Thai-Style Grilled Chicken

Inspired by Kenji's Thai-Style Grilled Chicken. I almost always add things to the marinade until it tastes good but this is a rough guideline.

Ingredients

Steps

Glorious Result

6 bone in chicken thighs (substitute ~2-3 lbs of your preferred cut; cooking times may differ--use a thermometer!)

Marinade Ingredients

  • 2 pucks of palm sugar (substitute ~2-3 tbsp brown sugar)
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • ~1 inch ginger
  • 1 thai chili (add more if you want it spicier)
  • ¼ tsp msg
  • 2-3 limes
  • 5-6 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp dark soy sauce
  1. Combine marinade ingredients into a paste. Best with a mortar & pestle but you can food process all of it instead
  2. Combine marinade with chicken
  3. Cover and marinate for 8-24 hours in fridge
  4. Preheat grill to 500
  5. Set half of grill to low (charcoal to one side or burners on low for propane) and cook chicken skin-side up over cool side of grill 20-30 minutes
  6. Finish over high heat (final temp ~160-170F for thighs)
  7. Serve with sticky rice, papaya salad, grilled vegetables, nam pla prik
